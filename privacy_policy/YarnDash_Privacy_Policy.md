We do not collect, use, or share any personal information or data from users of YarnDash.

*   **Offline Gameplay:** The App operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

### 2. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Since we do not collect any personal information, the App is intended to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
